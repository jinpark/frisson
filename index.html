THE UNFROZEN FROZEN DESSERT

Say hello to Frisson.

Created by at team of students in McGill University's Food Lab, Frisson is a shelf-stable frozen dessert fortified with fibre and low in fat. It's safe at room temperature, making it a more sustainable choice. Frisson placed third in the IFTSA & MARS Product Development Competition in 2014.
